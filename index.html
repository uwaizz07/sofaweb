Our Story

Where Craftsmanship
Meets Your Home

For over 25 years, JJ Sofa Decoraters has been transforming homes across India with handcrafted sofas and custom curtains that blend timeless quality with modern design.

At JJ Sofa Decoraters, we believe your home deserves the best. Since 1998, we've been crafting sofas and curtains that blend durability with elegance — every frame carved from solid hardwood, every stitch sewn with care by skilled artisans.

  • Handcrafted by master artisans with 10+ years of experience
  • 100% solid wood frames — no plywood, no particle board
  • Premium Italian and Indian upholstery fabrics to choose from
  • Eco-friendly manufacturing with sustainable practices
